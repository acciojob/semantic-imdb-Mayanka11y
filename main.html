Summary

Inception is a gripping science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It explores the adventures of Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who can invade dreams. Offered a chance at redemption, Cobb takes on the seemingly impossible mission of planting an idea deep into someone's mind while battling his own fractured psyche.
